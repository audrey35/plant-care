Top 3 Indoor Plants for Beginners

1. Golden Pothos Vine

The golden pothos grows well with minimal care. You can even raise them in a glass of water.

2. Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

The snake plant likes sunlight, but can handle less. They also don't require that much water.

3. Spider Plant

These plants require minimal maintenance, making them a popular choice for beginners. They don't require direct sunlight, but should be in a bright room. You should only water the plant when the soil is dry.
